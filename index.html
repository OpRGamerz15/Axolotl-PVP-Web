FAQ

How do I install Axolotl PVP+?
You can install it via Modrinth App, Prism Launcher, or ATLauncher by searching "Axolotl PVP+". For SKLauncher, follow our video tutorial.
What Minecraft version does it support?
Check the Modrinth page for the latest supported version. We keep the pack updated with new MC releases.
Can I use this on servers?
Yes! The modpack is designed for PVP servers. All mods are client-side and allowed on most competitive servers.
My FPS is still low — what do I do?
Allocate at least 4GB of RAM to Minecraft, disable shaders in-game, and make sure your GPU drivers are up to date. Check our Discord for extra tips.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Open an issue on our GitHub Issues page with as much detail as possible.
